Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

Introduction

Your Turn ("we", "our", or "the app") is a fun decision-making party game. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

Data We Collect

Your Turn collects minimal data:

  • Player names: Entered manually during gameplay. These are stored only on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.
  • Game history: Results are stored locally on your device to provide the history feature.
  • Language preference: Stored locally on your device.
  • Purchase information: Managed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment details.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal identification information (email, phone, address).
  • We do not track your location.
  • We do not use analytics or tracking SDKs.
  • We do not share any data with third parties.

Third-Party Services

  • RevenueCat: Used for managing in-app purchases and subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect anonymous purchase-related data. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple App Store: All purchases are processed by Apple. See Apple's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

Your Turn does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app is a party game intended for general audiences.

Data Retention

All data is stored locally on your device. Deleting the app removes all stored data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
